About Us

Empowering Students Through Technology

TinkerHub NSSCE is a campus community of NSS College of Engineering, Palakkad, powered by TinkerHub Foundation, a non-profit organization building a sustainable ecosystem for students to learn and grow in technology.

Our mission is to promote peer learning, hands-on skills development, and innovation among engineering students. We organize workshops, hackathons, tech talks, and community projects to foster a culture of learning and building together.

With a focus on emerging technologies like AI, Machine Learning, Web Development, IoT, and more, we aim to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry requirements, preparing students to be future-ready technologists.
